# Grow consulting revenue from $80k to $150k in 12 months

> EXAMPLE FILE. Demonstrates the `plans/` format. Replace with your own plans.

## North star
Lift consulting revenue from $80k/year (2025 baseline) to $150k/year by end of 2026, without compromising depth of engagement.

## Why now
The [[WORK/initiatives/acme-payments-rewrite|ACME engagement]] marks a shift from one-off audits to embedded retainers. If the ACME shape works, it's replicable — and the board-visibility framing (demonstrable Q2 milestone) is a durable lever for future pitches.

## Current state
Just started (2026-01-15). Only the ACME engagement is active. Two prospects warm but not yet engaged.

## Blockers & risks
- **Concentration risk**: if ACME is 3 days/week, it caps throughput. Must actively prospect while delivering.
- **Case study dependency**: the $150k target assumes inbound from a public case study. If Jane blocks the case study for IP reasons, rethink.
- **Personal bandwidth**: 3 days/week embedded + prospecting + personal admin = tight. Audit energy budget at 2026-03-01.
