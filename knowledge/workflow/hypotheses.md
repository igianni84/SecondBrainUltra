## Hypothesis: Meeting transcripts miss the first 30 seconds on Google Meet

Google Meet's Gemini-notes feature often starts transcribing after the intro exchange ("hey can you hear me / yes / okay let's start"), which can drop the first explicit agenda statement.

- Confirmations: 1/3
  - 2026-01-15: observed during the [[raw/meetings/2026-01-15-acme-kickoff|ACME kickoff]] — the transcript started with "Thanks for joining" but the intro ~15 seconds were missing.

**How to apply while a hypothesis**: cross-check the first decision in a meeting with an attendee's memory when it matters.

## Hypothesis: Embedded engagement = 3 days/week is the sweet spot for 2-month windows

When a client wants "embedded" depth with "advisory" flexibility, 3 days/week for 8–10 weeks gives enough continuity to land architecture decisions but enough gap to preserve outside perspective.

- Confirmations: 1/3
  - 2026-01-15: Jane Doe at ACME explicitly asked for this shape, called it "embedded, then dial down". Matches my prior engagements with Initech (2024) and Globex (2023), but not yet tracked in this wiki.

**How to apply while a hypothesis**: when scoping a new engagement, propose this shape as a default; note deviations.
