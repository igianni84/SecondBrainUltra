### Remote-first async beats meetings for status — from [[raw/articles/2026-01-10-remote-first-principles|Remote-First Principles]]
Jane Doe (ACME CTO) reports that at ACME they've replaced almost all status meetings with async digests. Their claim: writing is the great equalizer, meetings are for ambiguity resolution, not broadcast.

- Implications for my own workflow: when a client asks for a weekly sync, propose a written digest + a 30-min sync *only if needed* model before defaulting to an hour-long recurring call.
- Tension to resolve: some clients value the face time for relationship, not information. Don't be dogmatic.
